On 10th March 2022, in Mumbai, 100X is going to host the first annual 100X Investor Summit.

This will give all invited startup investor delegates unique access to the tech, investment trends & emerging entrepreneurs of India. Our intention with the event is not to make it like a vanity parade but mean business. We will be privileged to have you join the 100X mission of improving access to early-stage capital, educating investors and building an India focused startup community. At the conference, we will deep dive into booming tech, trends & sectors that will help discover unicorns. Get an opportunity to meet the hottest 100X startups while you listen to massively successful VC thought leaders from the startup world. Let’s build the best innovation ecosystem in India together.

What is unique about 100X Investor Summit is the fact that content is specially curated for early-stage investors be it angel investors, micro VCs, corporate venture capital or family offices.

100X mission is to remove the barriers to access to credible deals and make it accessible to a wider pool of investor groups. Get to meet & speak with some of the brightest minds in the country and learn about their investment thesis & process. We believe that the best investments happen because of relationships. 100X Investor Summit is an intimate event for active investors to have real conversations with Investors, CVC & Family Offices that have been hand-selected by 100X.

  • Angel Investors - Learn, Engage & Connect with top VCs from India
  • Corporations - Learn how to future-proof the enterprise business from disruptions.
  • Family Office - Opportunity to diversify the wealth portfolio with startups as an asset class.
  • VC Funds - Get access to early-stage deal flow from the early stage Investors.

This summit is designed to inspire exponential thinking.
